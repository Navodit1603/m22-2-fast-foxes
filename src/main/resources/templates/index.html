Intro

Welcome to DN Stonks!


Vision:


  • Teaching students at Del Norte about the stock market/crypto currency interactively.
  • Del Norte Stonks(DNS) will provide a first-hand experience with the US stock market and shareholding.
  • On the website, students will learn and develop their general financial literacy skills.

Stocks



  • A security that represents the ownership of a fraction of a corporation.
  • A way to raise money to fund growth, products and other initiatives.
  • Real earning potential comes from the compound interest you earn on long-term holdings.

Crypto



  • Collection of binary data which works as a medium of exchange.
  • Form of payment that can be exchanged online for goods and services.
  • Fix the problems of traditional currencies by putting the power in the currency holders' hands.
